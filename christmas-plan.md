## Christmas Day Timeline

### T-6:00 — The Early Shift
*If eating at 2pm, this is 8am*
- [ ] Turkey out of fridge (needs to come to room temp)
- [ ] Ham into Le Creuset with water, cider, herbs, cloves - bring to boil, then simmer
- [ ] **First drink: Buck's Fizz or coffee, your call**

### T-5:00 — Turkey Goes In
*If eating at 2pm, this is 9am*
- [ ] Turkey into OVEN 1 (calculate time based on weight - approx 20 mins per kg + 90 mins)
- [ ] Set timer
- [ ] Baste every 45 mins or so

### T-3:00 — Ham Glazing Time
*If eating at 2pm, this is 11am*
- [ ] Ham out of Le Creuset, drain, score the fat in diamonds
- [ ] Stud with cloves if you're fancy
- [ ] Maple syrup glaze all over
- [ ] Ham into OVEN 2 at 200°C for 30-40 mins until glazed and beautiful
- [ ] Check turkey, baste

### T-2:30 — Oven Tetris Begins
*If eating at 2pm, this is 11:30am*
- [ ] Check turkey - is it nearly done? (Juices run clear, 74°C in thickest part)
- [ ] If turkey done: out to rest (it can rest for up to 1.5 hours, covered in foil)
- [ ] OVEN 1 cranked to 200-220°C for roasties

### T-2:00 — Potatoes & Parsnips In
*If eating at 2pm, this is 12:00pm*
- [ ] Goose fat into roasting tray, into OVEN 1 to get smoking hot
- [ ] Parboiled potatoes and parsnips into the hot fat, season
- [ ] Back in oven - they need ~1 hour, turn halfway
- [ ] Red cabbage into a pan on low to reheat (add a splash of water)
- [ ] **Glass of wine: you've earned it**

### T-1:30 — Sides Prep
*If eating at 2pm, this is 12:30pm*
- [ ] Ham should be done - out to rest
- [ ] Pigs in blankets onto a tray, into OVEN 2
- [ ] Dates in bacon onto a tray, into OVEN 2 (or air fryer)
- [ ] Yorkshire puddings - if frozen, they need ~10-15 mins from frozen (hold off until T-0:30)

### T-1:00 — The Final Push
*If eating at 2pm, this is 1:00pm*
- [ ] Brussels with pancetta - fry the pancetta, add sprouts, cook until done (~15 mins)
- [ ] Carrots into steamer
- [ ] Check potatoes & parsnips - turn them
- [ ] Stuffing into oven to warm through (if frozen, needs longer)
- [ ] Gravy into pan, add turkey resting juices, keep warm

### T-0:30 — Nearly There
*If eating at 2pm, this is 1:30pm*
- [ ] Yorkshires into oven (need high heat, ~15 mins)
- [ ] Carrots done? Toss in butter and honey, keep warm
- [ ] Maple glaze on parsnips (brush with maple syrup, back in for final 10 mins)
- [ ] Start carving turkey
- [ ] Slice ham
- [ ] **Shout for someone to set the table**

### T-0:15 — Plating
*If eating at 2pm, this is 1:45pm*
- [ ] Everything out
- [ ] Yorkshires out (they wait for no one)
- [ ] Final check: Turkey ✓ Ham ✓ Roasties ✓ Parsnips ✓ Sprouts ✓ Carrots ✓ Red cabbage ✓ Yorkshires ✓ Pigs in blankets ✓ Stuffing ✓
- [ ] Gravy into boats
- [ ] Cranberry sauce & bread sauce onto table

### T-0:00 — EAT

- [ ] Drink
- [ ] Eat
- [ ] You did it
